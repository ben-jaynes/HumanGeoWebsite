Greece Population Policy

"Nationals of other countries holding a residence permit or residence card may stay in Greece for a period in excess of 90 days. Because of the strict immigration policy, it may be difficult to acquire a residence permit or any other long-stay immigration document. A list of permissible grounds for applying for a residence permit is available on the website of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs."

Level 2

  • There seems to be a little more males, but it is mostly even between genders

Level 3
  • There are less people of older ages becuase you are more prone to health risks
  • There are the most people at ages 35-55 since that is the working class
  • It seems to be mostly even between genders since the societal norms do not overly favor one gender over the other
  • There might be more of a dip for males at age 30 since males are more likely to move away to go and work somewhere else
Level 4
  • With there being a pretty steep decline before the age of 35, Greece might see a decline in working age population in the next 20 years
  • There seem to be a lot less people being born now, meaning Greece is relying more on wokers from other countries
  • Greece seems to have a good healthcare system, so when the big working class ages, there might be problems with capacity.
